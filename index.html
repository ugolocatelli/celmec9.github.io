The Ninth International Meeting on Celestial Mechanics

14-18 September 2026, San Martino al Cimino, Viterbo, Italy

CELMEC IX will take place at the Balletti Park Hotel in San Martino al Cimino, Viterbo, Italy, during the period 14-18 September 2026. The format will be in presence up to the maximum capacity of the hotel. Priority for attendance in person will be given to the speakers (long and short talks) and to the organizers.

CELMEC has reached an agreement with the international journal "Celestial Mechanics and Dynamical Astronomy", to ensure the publication of original papers presented at the meeting. See the pages of the Topical collections of CELMEC IX for submission and deadline:

"Analytical and semi-analytical results in Celestial Mechanics"

"Pioneering computational techniques in Dynamical Astronomy".

IMPORTANT: Prizes will be given to the best article(s) published in each Topical collection of "Celestial Mechanics and Dynamical Astronomy", see the "Prizes" sub-page.
