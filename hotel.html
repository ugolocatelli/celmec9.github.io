CELMEC IX Accommodation

CELMEC IX - Accommodation Information

Venue

CELMEC IX will take place at the Balletti Park Hotel in San Martino al Cimino (Viterbo, Italy).

Hotel website: https://www.balletti.com/

Room Reservation

Participants can reserve a room directly at the hotel by writing to info@balletti.com, specifying that the reservation is within the conference CELMEC 9.

Room Rates (city tax not included to pay on spot)

Room Type Price per Person per Day
Triple Room € 85,00
Double Room € 94,00
Single Room € 114,00

Included in all rates:

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, 2 coffee breaks per day, and WiFi

Booking Recommendation

Due to the limited number of single rooms, participants are encouraged to reserve double or triple rooms, specifying in the reservation the names of the roommates.

For any request please contact: celmec (at) mat.uniroma2.it
